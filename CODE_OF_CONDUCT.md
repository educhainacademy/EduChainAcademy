# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, friendly, diverse, and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Give and accept constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community and learners
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Use inclusive language
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behavior
- Any form of financial advice or securities promotion
- Price speculation or ROI claims related to EDU tokens

## Token Conduct

- EDU tokens are utility tokens for platform access only
- Do not promote EDU as an investment or financial instrument
- Do not make price predictions or yield claims
- Report any community members engaging in prohibited financial promotion

## Enforcement

Community moderators may remove content or ban users for violations. Serious violations will be reported to platform administrators.

## Reporting

Report violations to: conduct@educhain.academy
